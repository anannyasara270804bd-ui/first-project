About CIS Club

Connecting students with technology, innovation, and collaboration to create a brighter future together.

Our Mission

CIS Club aims to provide students with a platform to explore, innovate, and grow in the field of computing. We organize workshops, coding challenges, and collaborative projects to enhance practical skills and teamwork.

Our History

Established in 2010, CIS Club has been a pioneer in promoting technology awareness in our university. Over the years, we have hosted numerous tech events, competitions, and seminars to foster knowledge and community engagement.
